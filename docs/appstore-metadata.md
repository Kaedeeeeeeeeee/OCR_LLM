## Description (EN)

Cheese! OCR lets you instantly extract text from anything on your screen.

Press a keyboard shortcut, select an area, and the recognized text is copied to your clipboard — ready to paste. That's it.

**Key Features:**

- Instant screen capture with a customizable hotkey
- 100% on-device OCR powered by Apple Vision — no internet required
- Supports English, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean
- Auto-copy to clipboard — no extra steps
- Lightweight menu bar app — no Dock clutter
- OCR history — browse and search past results
- Zero data collection — your screenshots never leave your Mac

Cheese! OCR is perfect for grabbing text from images, PDFs, videos, presentations, or anything else on your screen that you can't easily copy.
