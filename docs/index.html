Frequently Asked Questions

The short answers to the most common questions.

Is Cheese! OCR free?
Cheese! OCR is a one-time paid purchase on the Mac App Store. There's no subscription and no in-app purchases — buy once and use it forever, across every Mac signed in with your Apple ID.
Does Cheese! OCR work offline?
Yes. All text recognition runs entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework. The app has no network entitlements at all, so it literally cannot send data anywhere — even if it wanted to.
What languages does Cheese! OCR recognize?
English, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, and Korean out of the box. Mixed scripts within the same capture (e.g. English mixed with Chinese) are handled correctly.
How is it different from macOS Live Text?
Live Text only works inside a handful of Apple apps (Preview, Photos, Safari). Cheese! OCR works anywhere on your screen — video players, conference apps, protected PDFs, remote desktops — through a single global keyboard shortcut.
What version of macOS do I need?
Cheese! OCR runs on macOS 13 Ventura and later, on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.
Can I change the keyboard shortcut?
Yes. Open the app's settings and record any combination you like. The default shortcut is ⇧⌘E.
Are my screenshots uploaded anywhere?
Never. Screenshots are processed in memory via Apple Vision and are never saved to disk or sent over the network. The app has zero network permissions.
